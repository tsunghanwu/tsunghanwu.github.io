<About Me/>

Hello! I'm an accomplished Staff Applications Engineer at Synopsys with extensive experience in the semiconductor industry. My passion lies in bridging the gap between process technology and design through advanced TCAD applications and machine learning.

My expertise involves developing and deploying TCAD applications for process modeling, flow emulation, and Design-Technology Co-Optimization (DTCO). At Lam Research, I successfully improved wafer-to-wafer etch depth variation by over 30% using regression-based ML models and developed multiple in-spec plasma etching processes for FinFET fabrication.

I am proficient in automating complex workflows using Python and TCL, modeling nano-scale plasma etching, and performing parasitic extraction. I thrive on partnering with R&D teams to validate cutting-edge features and providing expert technical support to ensure customer success.
